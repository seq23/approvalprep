ApprovalPrep Apartment Application Readiness Checklist

Use real documents only. ApprovalPrep does not contact landlords or guarantee approval.

1. Confirm the application requirements.
2. Gather identity and contact details.
3. Gather truthful income proof.
4. Organize rental history and address dates.
5. Prepare explanations only for facts you can support.
6. Keep a copy trail of anything you send.
