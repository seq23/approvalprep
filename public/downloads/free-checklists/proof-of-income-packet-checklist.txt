ApprovalPrep Proof of Income Packet Checklist

Use real documents only. ApprovalPrep does not create income documents or guarantee approval.

1. Identify the reviewer request.
2. List all income sources truthfully.
3. Gather pay stubs, statements, offer letters, or other proof you actually have.
4. Check dates, names, amounts, and source details.
5. Explain gaps or unusual income only with truthful context.
6. Keep a copy trail.
