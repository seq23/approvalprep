# Hands-Off Automation Policy

ApprovalPrep may auto-run low-risk mechanical fixes such as sitemap, canonical, metadata, internal-link suggestions, answer index refresh, and fixture traces.

Approval is required for regulated credit content, legal/trust pages, payment flow, download templates, prompt guardrails, source-backed claim changes, pricing, and new indexable pages. Unsafe claims are blocked.

---

# Safe Harbor Hands-Off Automation Addendum

Hands-off automation is allowed only inside guardrails.

Allowed:

- safe educational content autopublish
- Safe Harbor-regulated content autopublish after passing checks
- safe rewrites of known risky phrases
- disclosure insertion
- sitemap and index surface refresh
- metric/report rebuilds
- backlink handoff exports

Not allowed:

- autonomous legal advice
- autonomous legal-source trust
- paid PDF/DOCX public exposure
- fake provider results
- fake citation wins
- publishing hard-blocked claims
- changing Stripe/Cloudflare/secrets without approval
