# Plain Language Standard

Customer-facing copy should aim for a 6th grade American consumer reading level. Legal boundary copy may reach 8th grade only when needed for accuracy.

Use short sentences. Use common words. Break steps down. Explain credit and document terms. Do not sound like a law firm or a credit repair company.

Use `use`, not `utilize`. Use `help`, not `facilitate`. Use `start`, not `commence`. Use `documents` or `papers`, not `documentation`.
