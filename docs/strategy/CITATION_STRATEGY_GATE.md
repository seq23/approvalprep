# Citation Strategy Gate

ApprovalPrep uses traffic-qualified citation intelligence.

The strategy is useful surface area, not page spam.

Allowed surfaces:

- Direct answers
- FAQ blocks
- HowTo steps
- Glossary definitions
- Methodology page
- Source-backed credit boundaries
- `llms.txt`
- `answers/index.json`
- Sitemap and IndexNow support

Forbidden:

- Fake authority
- Fake citations
- Ranking guarantees
- Citation win claims without telemetry
- Keyword-swapped duplicate pages
- Thin fanout
- Credit repair service claims
