# ApprovalPrep Offering Standard

Every offering must pass this contract before it can remain visible in the public offering universe.

## Required Product Mapping

- The offering must belong to one active paid product.
- The offering must have a named customer situation, CTA, search intent, and risk boundary.
- The offering must have a docs review page in `docs/offerings/`.
- The offering must point to a reviewable PDF and DOCX file.

## Required Delivery Model

- Every paid product is a direct paid download.
- The free Studio is optional and cannot be required to buy, download, or use a paid kit.
- After verified Stripe checkout, the buyer unlocks the purchased kit PDF guide and editable DOCX file.
- The bundled PDF and DOCX must contain the names of every offering the kit claims to include.

## Required Consumer Usefulness

- A non-expert customer must understand what the offering helps them prepare.
- The offering must point to a clear next step.
- The offering must be bundled into a kit that has PDF and DOCX assets.

## Required Safety Boundaries

- True facts only.
- No fake documents.
- No third-party contact by ApprovalPrep.
- No legal, financial, lending, tax, or credit repair advice.
- No promise of approval, deletion, funding, housing, employment, or acceptance.

## Required Download Sections

Each paid kit download should include Start Here, Before You Fill This In, Step-by-Step Instructions, Fill-In Template, Self-Review Checklist, What to Attach, What to Do Next, and What ApprovalPrep Does Not Do.

## Proof Boundary

The structural validator proves mapping, presence, direct-download model, boundaries, and review surfaces. It does not replace a human reading every PDF/DOCX before a major commercial launch.
