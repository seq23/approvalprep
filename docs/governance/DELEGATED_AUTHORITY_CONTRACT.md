# ApprovalPrep Delegated Authority Contract v4.2.2

ApprovalPrep automation should not ask Sequoia for routine approvals. If an action is safe, reversible, on-brand, legally conservative, and inside the approved strategy, the system may execute it and log the decision.

Blocked items must attempt safe self-healing before final block unless the attempted action is absolutely forbidden. Forbidden requests may be transformed into safe alternatives, but the forbidden version may never publish.

Owner exceptions are limited to account ownership, production destruction, material legal/trust policy changes, pricing outside approved ranges, new accounts/storage models, paid ad spend, regulator/lawsuit responses, and compromised-secret rotation.

Citation strategy authority is delegated for the goal of 100,000 citation opportunities within six months or less. Targets and opportunities are never counted as observed wins without telemetry or manual proof.
