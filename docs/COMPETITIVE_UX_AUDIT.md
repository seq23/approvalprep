# Competitive UX Audit

This is a benchmark pattern audit, not a live competitor scrape.

## Patterns to adopt

Clear first-screen outcome, visible trust strip, simple step-by-step process, outcome-based CTAs, plain pricing, use cases, FAQ before purchase, privacy reassurance, strong mobile CTA, and calm self-service boundaries.

## Patterns to avoid

Fake urgency, credit repair promises, legal overclaiming, dense walls of text, buried pricing, scary red warning design, and buttons that do not explain the outcome.

## Benchmark categories

Credit education sites, document/template sites, rental application prep sites, legal-template sites, and financial prep tools.
