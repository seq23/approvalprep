# ApprovalPrep Product Catalog v2

ApprovalPrep launches with 8 Stripe products that cover the full self-service catalog. This avoids 30 separate checkout buttons while still selling the full offer universe.

Each paid product is one direct paid download: a PDF guide plus an editable DOCX file. The named offerings are the templates, checklists, and use cases inside those bundled kit files. The free Studio is optional and is not required to buy, download, or use any paid kit.
