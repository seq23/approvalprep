# Proof of Income Letter Generator

## What this is

This is a self-service ApprovalPrep download. It helps you prepare your own paperwork.

## When to use it

Use it when you need a clear letter, checklist, or packet before you apply or respond.

## What to review before sending

1. Check every name.
2. Check every date.
3. Check every dollar amount.
4. Remove anything that is not true.
5. Add proof when you have it.

## What to attach

Attach only real records that match your situation.

## Where to send it

Send it yourself to the landlord, lender, employer, creditor, or office that requested it.

## What to do next

1. Save a copy.
2. Send the packet.
3. Track the date.
4. Follow up if needed.

## What this does not do

This does not guarantee approval. It does not create fake documents. It does not send anything for you.
