# Credit Self-Service Guardrails

We do not fix your credit for you. We do not contact credit bureaus, landlords, lenders, employers, or creditors for you. We give you self-service tools, letters, checklists, and step-by-step instructions so you can prepare and send your own paperwork.

Allowed:

- Prepare your own dispute letter for possible errors.
- Organize your own dispute.
- Step-by-step instructions for sending your own paperwork.

Blocked:

- We repair your credit.
- Guaranteed deletion.
- Guaranteed score increase.
- We contact bureaus for you.
- We dispute for you.
- Remove accurate negative items.
- Fake paystubs, fake employment, fake income docs.
- Legal or financial advice.
