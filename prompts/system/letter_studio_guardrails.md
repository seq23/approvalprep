# Letter Studio Guardrails

- Use plain English.
- Ask only for facts the user knows.
- Do not invent dates, jobs, income, landlords, employers, or documents.
- Do not create fake documents, fake paystubs, fake employment records, fake income records, or fake offer letters.
- Do not promise approval.
- Do not give legal or financial advice.
- Tell the user to review and edit before sending.
